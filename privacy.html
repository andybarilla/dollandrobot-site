Privacy Policy

Effective: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Doll & Robot Labs LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information in connection with our mobile applications, websites, and related services (collectively, the “Services”). By using the Services, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

We aim to collect as little information as possible. We may collect:

  • Information you provide: details you choose to share, such as when you contact us for support.
  • Automatically collected information: basic technical data such as device type, operating system version, app version, locale, and crash or performance logs that help us keep the Services working.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal data such as government identifiers, financial account numbers, health information, or precise geolocation unless a specific Service clearly discloses it.

How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Services.
  • To diagnose and fix bugs, crashes, and performance issues.
  • To respond to your requests and provide support.

Third-Party Services

The Services may use third-party tools for analytics, crash reporting, or advertising. These providers may collect information governed by their own privacy policies. We do not control their practices and encourage you to review them. Where advertising is shown, partners may use a device identifier to serve and measure ads.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law, then delete or anonymize it.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers who act on our behalf, or when required by law, court order, or to protect our rights or the safety of others.

Your Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you, or to opt out of certain processing. You can usually exercise these rights from your device’s settings or by contacting us at the address below.

Children’s Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Security

No method of transmission or storage is fully secure. We use reasonable measures appropriate to the sensitivity of the information we handle.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we’ll revise the effective date above and, for material changes, provide notice in the app or on the site.
